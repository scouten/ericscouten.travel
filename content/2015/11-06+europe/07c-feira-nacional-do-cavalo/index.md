We timed our visit this week to take in the [National Horse Fair (English)](https://feiradagolega.com/en/) or [Feira Nacional do Cavalo (Portuguese)](https://feiranacionaldocavalo.com). It's an amazing spectacle centered around the Lusitano horse, all manner of riding attire, and roasted chestnuts.

<!-- more -->

My friend Keron and her husband (2026 update: Carlos has since passed) are very active in this community and they were most gracious hosts in introducing us to this evening's festivities.
