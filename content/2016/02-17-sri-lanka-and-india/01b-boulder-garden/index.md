We are staying this evening in the [Boulder Garden Resort](https://www.facebook.com/Bouldergarden2016), a two-hour drive from Negombo and the fish market.

<!-- more -->

We've had most of the afternoon to relax and explore the unique grounds here which have been carved out of large boulders from the area.
